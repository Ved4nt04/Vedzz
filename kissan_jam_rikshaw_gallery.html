Kissan Jam

Kissan Jam is a popular fruit jam brand loved across India. Known for its sweet and tangy mixed fruit flavor, it’s a breakfast favorite — perfect on bread, rotis, or even as a dessert topping.

Rikshaw

The auto-rikshaw, often called a “tuk-tuk,” is one of India’s most iconic modes of transport. Colorful, quick, and efficient, it’s a symbol of urban mobility found in nearly every city.
